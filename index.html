Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my Bybit account?

To verify your Bybit account, go to 'Account & Security' -> 'Identity Verification'. You'll need to upload a valid ID document and complete facial recognition. This typically takes a few minutes.

What payment methods are supported for deposits?

Bybit supports P2P trading with various local bank transfers. You can also use One-Click Buy with bank transfers or certain credit/debit cards (subject to issuer restrictions).

How long do withdrawals take?

Crypto withdrawals are processed quickly, usually within 30 minutes to an hour. Fiat (local currency) withdrawals via P2P depend on the merchant and bank, but are generally fast.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your registered email or phone number to reset it. Follow the instructions sent to you.
